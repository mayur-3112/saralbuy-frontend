# Brag Plan

## 9-Question Rubric

1. **What is the app?**
   Quotex by SaralBuy is a B2B materials exchange platform for sourcing industrial and construction materials via RFQs.

2. **What is the funniest or most impressive claim?**
   Sourcing massive quantities of heavy materials (like 1500 bags of UltraTech OPC 53 Grade Cement or 12 Tons of TMT Rebars) through a slick, modern UI.

3. **What is the visual hook?**
   A clean, modern interface featuring the `Geist Variable` font, subtle rounded corners, and a sharp orange accent (`rgb(235, 120, 19)` / `text-orange-600`). 

4. **What should be shown from the actual UI?**
   The Product Overview page showing the comprehensive "Requirement Specifications" grid we just built, followed by the massive 45vw "Submit Quotation" drawer sliding in from the right.

5. **What is the shortest satisfying video?**
   About 15 seconds. Hook -> Show Grid -> Slide Drawer -> Call to action.

6. **What tone fits best?**
   `app-store` - smooth, feature-card clean. It's a B2B corporate app, but we want it to feel modern and premium.

7. **What should the audio feel like?**
   Warm corporate bed with slick, modern SFX matching the drawer sliding and button clicks.

8. **What should the share caption say?**
   "Sourcing 1500 bags of cement just got as easy as ordering a pizza. Meet the new Quotex by SaralBuy."

9. **What's the user flow worth showing?**
   View RFQ requirements grid -> Click "Place Quote Now" -> Huge drawer slides out for submitting a bid.

---

## Storyboard

**Music Cue:** Corporate, upbeat electronic bed.

### Scene 1: The Hook (0s - 3s)
- **Visual:** Solid dark background (`#1A1A1A`). Big bold text in Geist: "B2B Sourcing used to be slow." Text wipes away. Next text: "Not anymore."
- **Audio:** Music drops in. Gentle bass swoosh.

### Scene 2: The Grid Reveal (3s - 7s)
- **Visual:** Cut to the white UI of the Product Overview. The camera zooms out slowly to reveal the "Requirement Specifications" grid showing tags like "Category: Building Materials", "Estimated Budget: ₹380", and "Quantity: 1500 Bags".
- **Audio:** Series of tiny, high-tech `pop` sounds as the grid items stagger in.

### Scene 3: The Action (7s - 11s)
- **Visual:** Focus on the "Place Quote Now" button. Cursor clicks it. The massive side drawer (45vw width) slides smoothly in from the right side of the screen with the title "Submit Quotation".
- **Audio:** Mouse click SFX, followed by a smooth, deep `woosh` for the drawer sliding.

### Scene 4: Outro / Punchline (11s - 15s)
- **Visual:** Fade to solid orange (`#EB7813`). White text centers on screen: "Quotex by SaralBuy. Start sourcing today."
- **Audio:** Music swells to a finish and cuts out.
